My story

Hi, my name is John Doe and I work as a QA Engineer Director at Microsoft. In my spare time, I'm writing this food blog, testing and inventing recipes, and cooking - all different cuisines.

I started cooking at a young age - as a boarding school student, I couldn't afford a delivery service, so I had no choice but to learn how to cook. Sure, in the beginning it was instant food, sausages and similar junk food but over time, I worked my way up to fresh ingredients.

My grandfather helped me the most with cooking and taught me the absolute basics that I couldn't handle. From slicing onions and peeling potatoes to the basics of sauces and roasting meat. I remember we started with simple spaghetti, with onions, garlic, soy meat (it was a cheap alternative to meat, which I appreciated very much as a student) and tomato sauce. I love this recipe and it was my favorite for a long time.

Pasta formed the cornerstone of my kitchen - spaghetti, pasta salads, pasta and sauce mix. Over time my grandfather taught me other dishes, meat and potatoes, steaks, chicken popcorn and many more recipes.
With these basics in mind, I was cooking for my friends by the time I was in my second year of high school, earning a few extra dollars, and it was here that my love of cooking began, which has progressed to the point where you are reading this blog.

I hope you like my recipes and I will be glad if you share your recipe with me.
You can send me any comments to my email using the form on contact page, or contact me on social media.
